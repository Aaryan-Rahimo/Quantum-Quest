Grade 9 Topics

Kinematics
Explore the principles of motion, including velocity, acceleration, and the fundamental equations that describe how objects move. This topic covers everything you need to understand the basics of kinematics.
Forces
Forces are interactions that cause changes in an object's motion. They include gravity, friction, and tension, and are described by Newton's Laws of Motion.
Energy
Energy is the ability to do work or cause change. It exists as kinetic, potential, and other forms, and is conserved in all physical processes.
Waves
Waves are disturbances that carry energy through a medium or space. They include mechanical waves like sound and electromagnetic waves like light.
Electromagentism
Electromagnetism is the study of electric and magnetic fields and their interactions. It covers phenomena like electricity, magnetism, and electromagnetic waves.
